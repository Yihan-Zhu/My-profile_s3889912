My ideal project

This project is original to build an APP to help personal gourmet or food lover to record and classify delicious food information which including more than the restaurant location and evaluate for restaurant food, the cooking method and food source for home cooking correct to every single dish name. Whenever the ideas come out, then you can directly find where or how to get a delicious food in the restaurant or cooking by yourself. The APP dedicate to help people develop a habit that record and share their experience in specific way not only for the restaurant but also for single dish like food blogger and chef so that every people can become a senior gastronome when you share your experience with your friends or people want to make their life more colourful after a long-term accumulation by using this APP.

In this APP, I deicide to provide an innovative way to prepare a big meal for food lover using APP. In the APP, a big meal can be prepared from a detailed idea such a dish name, food name or flavour first, and we can add more details to filtrate the food which we want to eat in the meal. Then it can list all the relevant restaurant and cooking methods sort out by location, waiting time, price of materials or per capita, services, and means of purchase or even more. It maybe looks a little bit mussy, but we can set these screening approach into two main parts. So, the first prat provides the basic function to encourage and help food lover to record the food experience by specific template which including the dish name, restaurant information, location, price, and comments for the restaurant food. Also, in second part, I want to combine the methods way and food sources for home cooking with comments on restaurant food so that people can have more choices at the same time by using this APP. Hence, another plate should be set as cuisine preparation in the APP. This plate plan to add a special template to record the cooking methods with experience and the food source which including different suggestion purchase way, the method of preservation and some tips. To encourage people to use this APP to gather more experience, I decide to add some function such as one-click upload to share the diet information on some mainstream social software which including Tweet, Facebook, Wechat Monments and so on. Meanwhile, I still provide some optional function can be exploited if there are still available time and condition to achieve more functions in this project. For example, I decide to add a healthy diet monitoring function which comes from the project in User-centred design to help people focus on their diet healthy while enjoying the food by using this APP.

For building the APP, the MIT APP inventor is a good choice for this project. However, there may have some functions in the APP which are not easy to finish by the MIT APP inventor individually. So, the python programming language is also a choice when I make this project. Also, the optional function needs to the data from the User-centre design. To complete this project, a computer and one mobile of IOS or Android are necessary. And some software like Vscode, Github, and MIT APP inventor can make our work more efficient.

In the process of building the APP, there are some necessary skills which including the basic programming ability for python and be skilled in using MIT APP inventor.
